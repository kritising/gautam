Our Story

M/S Gautam Construction is a registered firm under the Indian partnership Act U/S 58(1) of 1932(Reg No. 64) corporate by its Managing Partner Shri Mithilesh Kumar whose involved in Construction business since2001 in various civil works in Odisha, Bihar & Jharkhand.

We believe in building value adding relationship with our customers in the following ways;

  • To create employment
  • To preserve environment
  • To participate in the construction, rehabilitation, repair and maintenance of roads of all kinds.

Why Choose Us

Our team of dedicated and highly skilled professionals will carefully guide you through each step of making roads & bridge. We believe the key to an enjoyable and rewarding experience is best achieved by ensuring that open and honest communication is maintained throughout all aspects. This philosophy is applied from your initial enquiry to well after the completion and handover of your project, ensuring you total peace of mind.
Offering our clients a flexible and detailed service, we are able to manage all aspects of the building process to the highest possible standards. This includes working with local councils, initial concept and design through to approvals and construction of your project. Our team of professionals will work closely with you each step of the way, ensuring the finished project is exactly as you intended. Committed to providing a truly unique and rewarding road experience for our clients, we adopt the latest technologies and creative design principles to transform your ideas into reality. Our dedicated trades are of superior quality and fine workmanship and like us, pride themselves on delivering environments that are truly inspiring - Roads of the highest standards, tailored to your specific requirements.
